About Our Centre

The Isleworth Deen Centre is a mosque and community center located at 114 Twickenham Road, Isleworth, London. Established to serve the Muslim community in West London, we provide a place for worship, education, and community gatherings.

Our mission is to promote Islamic values, foster community spirit, and provide a welcoming environment for people of all backgrounds to learn about Islam and engage in interfaith dialogue.

We offer daily prayers, Friday Jumu'ah congregation, Quran classes for children and adults, Islamic lectures, and various community support services.

Our Services

Daily Prayers

All five daily prayers are held at the mosque with congregation. Separate prayer areas for men and women.

Quran Classes

Quran reading and memorization classes for children and adults with qualified teachers.

Friday Prayer

Jumu'ah prayer with Khutbah in English and Arabic. Multiple sessions to accommodate everyone.

Community Support

Marriage services, counseling, funeral services, and community outreach programs.
